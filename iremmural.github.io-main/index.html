Motor Vehicle Collisions Analysis in New York

Analyzing NYC Motor Vehicle Collisions to Uncover Safety Insights

Research Questions

Descriptive Analysis (Q1)

How does the frequency of accidents vary by time of day and different boroughs of New York? This study identifies high-risk "danger hours" and "danger zones" to understand temporal and spatial patterns.

Descriptive Analysis (Q2)

Is there a relationship between crash causes and vehicle types that increase severity? We examine the combined effects of behaviors (e.g., speeding) and vehicle characteristics.

Predictive Modeling (Q3)

Can we predict the number of injuries using variables like time, location, and vehicle type? This builds a regression model to assess the feasibility of estimating the human cost of a crash.
